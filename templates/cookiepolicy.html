This website uses cookies for two purposes: 1) to ensure CSRF protection when submitting a project 2) as a simple deterrent against a single user voting for a project more than once. These cookies are not used to track the user in any way. Only the CSRF token is used on the server, the voting-related cookies are not looked at even once in server-land.
